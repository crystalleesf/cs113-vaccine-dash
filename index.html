State of the world

The year is 2020 and Covid-19 has disrupted the world. LeShawna lives with her family in Wawanakwa, a corrupt country with a clear wealth divide which often leaves families like LeShawna's neglected. Although vaccine distribution has begun, the government is restricting access to the wealthy and those who are willing to pay a high price for it.

Fight for justice

LeShawna needs your help! LeShawna doesn't have the means to buy vaccines and she fears that without the vaccine, her whole family will die. She has already lost her father to Covid, and her mom has an autoimmune disorder, putting her at high risk. She also lives with her husband, who is a teacher at the neighborhood school, and two kids who dream of becoming doctors when they grow up.

LeShawna has decided to take matters into her own hands and steal the vaccines from the local hospital.

Help LeShawna navigate the hospital to find the vaccines that'll save her family!
